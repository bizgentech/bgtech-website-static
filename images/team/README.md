# Team Member Photos

This folder contains professional photos of BG Tech team members.

## Image Guidelines

### Team Member Headshots
- **Recommended Size:** 800x800px (square) or 600x800px (portrait)
- **Format:** JPG or WebP
- **Max File Size:** 200KB per photo
- **Background:** Neutral, professional (white, gray, or office environment)
- **Quality:** High-resolution, well-lit, professional

### Team Group Photos
- **Recommended Size:** 1600x900px (landscape)
- **Format:** JPG
- **Max File Size:** 500KB
- **Content:** Team gatherings, office culture, collaborative work

## Photo Requirements

### Individual Headshots
- **Framing:** Shoulders and up
- **Expression:** Professional, friendly, approachable
- **Attire:** Business casual or professional
- **Consistency:** Similar lighting, background, and framing across all photos
- **Resolution:** Sharp, in-focus

### Group Photos
- **Setting:** Office environment, team events, collaborative spaces
- **Quality:** Professional photography preferred
- **Authenticity:** Genuine team interactions

## Naming Convention

Use clear, consistent names:
- `{firstname-lastname}.jpg` - Individual headshots
- `{firstname-lastname}-alt.jpg` - Alternative poses
- `team-group-{year}.jpg` - Group photos
- `team-office-{description}.jpg` - Office environment

Examples:
- `john-doe.jpg`
- `jane-smith.jpg`
- `team-group-2024.jpg`
- `team-office-collaboration.jpg`
